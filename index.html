Drag Chain

A drag chain is a flexible carrier that guides and protects cables or hoses in motion, preventing wear and tangling. We manufacture both polymer and steel drag chains— polymer drag chains are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and ideal for high-speed, low-load applications, while steel drag chains offer high strength and durability for heavy-duty or harsh industrial environments.

Cable Reeling Drums

Cable reeling drums manage and store cables during movement. Available in spring-loaded and motorised types, they ensure safe, efficient cable handling in dynamic applications across industrial and mobile equipment setups.

Cables

Industrial cables transmit power, signals, or data in factories, machinery, and automation systems. They are designed for durability, flexibility, and resistance to heat, oil, chemicals, and mechanical stress.

Conduit pipes

A conduit pipe is a protective tubing used to route and shield electrical wires in industrial, commercial, or residential settings, offering safety, durability, and resistance to moisture, heat, and impact.
